If you click on your company logo in the top left corner, this will take you back to your Home page where your Home Task columns are displayed. The Client Menu will give you access to Client related searches. The Diary Menu will link through to the your Diary page. The Suppliers Menu is there for you to search your Suppliers as well as PTS contracted suppliers. The Accounts Menu will allow you to view and manage your payments and transactions. The Member Menu allows quick access to Member related pages including Reporting tools depending on your User Privilege levels.

This page allows you to search for a client or clients by any combination of the fields here. Your Search Results will display at the bottom of the page and you can click through to each Client via their client reference, first name or last name. You can also click through to the Money page of the relevant client by clicking on their Credits, Debits or Balance values. All information in the results can then be exported to  Excel, PDF, printed or copied to clipboard if required.
